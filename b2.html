The Speed You Need.

A day’s power in half an hour.

OnePlus Fast Charge offers a faster kind of fast charging. It’s not just fast when the screen is off. It also maintains its incredibly fast charging speed while you’re using your OnePlus 6, so you don’t have to pause that movie to charge up.

More RAM, faster performance.

Everything you do on your OnePlus 6 uses RAM. More RAM means more speed. Watch a video, play a game and browse social media, then switch between them in an instant. We want to speed up your experience, so you can do everything you want to do in less time. That’s why we love RAM.
